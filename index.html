About Dev Kenneth, LLC

Trusted life insurance guidance for individuals and families.

Dev Kenneth, LLC is a licensed life insurance agency based in Pinellas Park, Florida. We specialize in helping individuals and families find the right life insurance coverage to protect their loved ones and secure their financial future.

We understand that choosing life insurance can feel overwhelming. That's why we take a personalized approach — listening to your needs, explaining your options in plain language, and recommending coverage that fits your budget and goals.

Whether you're looking for term life insurance, whole life coverage, or final expense protection, Dev Kenneth, LLC is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Services

We offer a range of life insurance products to meet your unique needs.

🛡

Term Life Insurance

Affordable coverage for a set period — 10, 20, or 30 years. Ideal for protecting your family during your working years.

🏠

Whole Life Insurance

Lifetime coverage with a cash value component that grows over time. A policy that works as both protection and savings.

💖

Final Expense Insurance

Designed to cover funeral costs and end-of-life expenses so your family isn't burdened during a difficult time.

📈

Mortgage Protection

Ensures your mortgage is paid off if something happens to you, keeping your family in their home.
